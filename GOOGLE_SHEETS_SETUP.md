# Google Sheets Setup — 30 Seconds

## Quick Import (Easiest)

### Step 1: Download CSV
```
Go to: https://github.com/Deji147x/parlevu-global-services-llc
Download: BUFFER_POSTS.csv
```

### Step 2: Create New Google Sheet
1. Go to **Google Sheets** (sheets.google.com)
2. Sign in with **parlevugloballlc@gmail.com**
3. Click **Create** → **Blank spreadsheet**
4. Name it: **"Parlevu Buffer Posts"**

### Step 3: Import CSV
1. Click **File** → **Import**
2. Click **Upload** tab
3. Select **BUFFER_POSTS.csv** from your Downloads
4. Choose **Replace current sheet**
5. Click **Import**

**Done!** Your sheet is ready. ✅

## How to Use It

### For Posting to Buffer:

1. **Open Google Sheet** (Parlevu Buffer Posts)
2. **Find a post** you want to share (or go in order)
3. **Copy the Caption** (entire cell from "Caption" column)
4. **Go to Buffer.com** → **Compose**
5. **Paste caption** into text area
6. **Paste URL** from "Blog URL" column
7. **Schedule** for Mon/Wed/Fri at 9 AM
8. **Mark row as done** (add checkmark or change status)

### Example:
```
Post #5: Facing Foreclosure in Washington DC...
Caption: "Facing foreclosure in Washington DC? You have options..."
URL: https://www.parlevugloballlc.com/blog-foreclosure-dc.html
→ Schedule to Buffer → Done ✓
```

## Sorting & Filtering (Optional)

### Sort by State
1. Click **Data** → **Create a filter**
2. Click filter icon on **State** column
3. Choose **MD**, **VA**, or **DC** to focus on one state

### Search for Topics
1. Press **Ctrl+F**
2. Search for keywords: "foreclosure", "city name", "inherited", etc.
3. Find matching posts instantly

### Sort by Post Number
Click **Data** → **Sort range** → **Post #** (Ascending)

## Data Fields Explained

**Post #:** Posting order (1-73)
- Start with #1, go in sequence
- Or jump around and post your favorites first

**Title:** Full blog post title
- Use for reference when choosing what to post

**State:** MD (Maryland), VA (Virginia), DC (Washington DC)
- Useful for themed posting weeks
- E.g., "Maryland Week" = posts 1, 4, 7, etc.

**Caption:** Pre-written Buffer caption
- Copy this exactly
- Includes: hook, value prop, hashtags
- Formatted for best engagement

**Blog URL:** Full link to live blog post
- Paste into Buffer after caption
- Buffer will auto-pull featured image
- Readers click to read full article

**Status:** "Published" or "Generated"
- Published = Posted to website already
- Generated = Waiting in queue
- All are ready to share to social

## FAQ

**Q: Can I edit the captions?**
A: Yes! Feel free to customize captions for your audience. Make them your own.

**Q: Can I reorder the posts?**
A: Yes, sort the sheet however you want. Posting order is flexible.

**Q: Can I share this with my team?**
A: Yes! Click Share → Add their emails.

**Q: What if a caption doesn't work?**
A: Edit it in the sheet, or rewrite from scratch. All 73 are starting points.

**Q: How often should I post?**
A: Recommended: 3x/week (Mon/Wed/Fri at 9 AM) = 6 min/week investment.

**Q: What if I miss a week?**
A: No problem! Posts don't expire. Post whenever you're ready.
