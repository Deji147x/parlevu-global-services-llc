Why Arlington Homeowners Choose Direct Cash Sales

We've closed over 100 deals across Maryland, Virginia, and DC. Arlington is our backyard. We know the market — what homes sell for, what repair costs actually are, and how to close fast without the agent middleman taking 5-6%.

Most sellers we talk to have already listed for 30-60 days with zero offers. Or they're dealing with a situation that won't wait: foreclosure breathing down their neck, inherited property sitting vacant, or a property that needs $30K in repairs just to pass inspection.

Here's the thing: traditional buyers using bank financing can't touch distressed properties. Appraisers kill the deal. That's why our buyer pool is almost entirely investors and cash buyers — people who can close on anything.

How We Buy Arlington Houses — The Process

Step 1: You call or fill out our form. Tell us about the property — address, condition, any special circumstances. Two minutes, max.

Step 2: We inspect it (usually the same day or next morning). We're not sending appraisers or financing contingencies — we're pricing what we see and what the market will bear.

Step 3: We send a written offer. This is binding on us, non-binding on you. You review it. Most sellers say yes on the spot.

Step 4: We close on your timeline. Seven days? We can do that. Thirty days? Even better — gives you time to plan. We handle all closing costs. No surprises.

What Happens to Your Arlington Home After Closing

Honestly? We either renovate it and resell, or we hold it as a rental. Depends on the property and the market. Either way, you don't have to think about it anymore. It's not your problem.

That's the real value of selling to us. No more property taxes, insurance, maintenance, tenant headaches, or vacant house decay. One check. Done.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much less will I get selling for cash vs. listing in Arlington?

Cash buyers typically offer 70–85% of a home's after-repair value (ARV) to account for repair costs, carrying costs, and our margin. But you save 5-6% in agent commissions and avoid paying for repairs, staging, and a 2-3 month carrying cost. Net difference is often smaller than you'd think.

Do you really buy any condition?

Yes. Fire damage, water damage, mold, foundation issues, occupied by tenants, full of junk — we've bought all of it. The price reflects the condition, but the offer is real and we close.

What if I owe more than my Arlington house is worth?

We can work with that. Short sale situations are common. We handle the lender negotiations; you walk away debt-free. No deficiency on your credit.
