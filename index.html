Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Or join our Google Meet for a live conversation.

How quickly can I receive a cash offer?
+
After you contact us with basic details about your property, we typically present a no-obligation cash offer within 24–48 hours. We can often move even faster if needed — call us at (667) 646-8306 to discuss your timeline.
Do I need to make any repairs before selling?
+
Absolutely not. We purchase homes in any condition — fire damage, water damage, outdated systems, code violations, or simply years of wear and tear. You don't lift a finger or spend a single dollar on repairs.
Are there any fees or commissions?
+
None whatsoever. There are no real estate agent commissions, no closing costs, and no hidden fees. The cash offer we make is the amount you receive at closing — and we cover all standard closing costs.
Can we meet virtually instead of in person?
+
Yes! We offer full virtual consultations via Google Meet. You can discuss your property, ask questions, and even complete parts of the process entirely online. Join our Google Meet anytime during business hours, or schedule a time that works for you.
What areas do you serve?
+
We primarily serve Baltimore City and Baltimore County, but also work throughout the greater DMV area including Maryland suburbs, Washington DC, and Northern Virginia. Contact us to confirm your location.
What if I'm behind on mortgage payments or facing foreclosure?
+
We specialize in helping homeowners in difficult situations, including pre-foreclosure, late payments, and distressed properties. Our fast cash closings can often stop a foreclosure and get you cash before the bank acts. Contact us immediately at (667) 646-8306.
