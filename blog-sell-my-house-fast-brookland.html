Do I Need to Disclose Anything When Selling My House?

If you're selling your house in Brookland, DC, it's essential to disclose certain information to potential buyers. For homes built before 1978, federal lead-paint disclosure duties apply, and a Seller's Property Condition Disclosure is usually required for most residential sales.

What Brookland Homeowners Should Know First

DC requires a Seller's Property Condition Disclosure for most residential sales, and pre-1978 homes carry federal lead-paint disclosure duties.
