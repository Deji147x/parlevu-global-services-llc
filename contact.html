Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?
+
It's simple — fill out the form above, call us at (667) 646-8306, or join our Google Meet for an immediate virtual consultation. All options are free with zero obligation.
Is the cash offer really free and no-obligation?
+
100%. We will never charge you for a consultation or an offer. You're completely free to decline our offer at any time, for any reason — no pressure, no penalty.
What information do I need to provide?
+
Just basic details — property address, your contact information, and a brief description of the property's condition and your situation. You don't need any documents upfront. We'll request anything additional after reviewing your submission.
Can I get an offer if I live out of state?
+
Absolutely. We work with out-of-state owners of Baltimore-area properties all the time. Our virtual Google Meet process makes it easy to handle everything remotely — from the initial consultation to reviewing and signing documents. Book a Google Meet and we'll guide you through the entire process.
How long does the entire process take?
+
Once you contact us, we can typically present a cash offer within 24–48 hours. If you accept, we can close in as few as 7 days or on whatever timeline works best for you. The average from first contact to closing is 14–21 days.
Do you serve areas outside of Baltimore City?
+
Yes. We serve all of Baltimore City, Baltimore County, and the broader DMV area including Maryland suburbs, Washington DC, and Northern Virginia. Contact us with your address to confirm we cover your area.
What types of properties do you buy?
+
We purchase single-family homes, multi-family properties (duplexes, triplexes, apartment buildings), condos, townhouses, and vacant land in the Baltimore/DMV area. We consider all property conditions including distressed, fire-damaged, or properties with title issues.
