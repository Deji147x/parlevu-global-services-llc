Our Story

Baltimore-Born. Community-Driven.

Parlevu Global Services LLC was founded in 2022 with a clear mission: give Baltimore homeowners a better way to sell. We saw too many families stuck in difficult housing situations — facing foreclosure, dealing with inherited properties, or simply unable to afford costly repairs before listing — with no good options available to them.

As a Black-owned and indigenous-owned business, we are deeply committed to serving the communities we come from. We understand the unique challenges Baltimore homeowners face, and we built our process around making your experience as simple, dignified, and profitable as possible.

We are verified by Google, licensed to operate throughout Maryland, and proud to offer both in-person consultations at our Baltimore office and virtual meetings via Google Meet for homeowners who prefer the convenience of connecting from anywhere.
